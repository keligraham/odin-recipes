K's Chibom

Ghanaian Bread and Egg

This is one of my favouritethings to make because it is low effort and does not use up that many ingredients. Just toss up and fry up and good to go!

Ingredients

  • Koala/Shoprite Bread
  • Large eggs
  • Anapuna Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Sausages
  • Corned beef (Optional)
  • Butter/oil
